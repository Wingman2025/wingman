IWO Wing Foiling Course Levels

Your complete guide to mastering wing foiling from beginner to advanced

The International Wing Foiling Organization (IWO) defines a clear progression for wing foil students, from first steps on the beach (Level 1A) up to advanced maneuvers (Level 4T). Each level corresponds to specific skills you must learn before moving on.

This guide breaks down every level 1A through 4T, explaining each skill in simple terms, with technical tips, best practices, and common mistakes to avoid. We've included links to official IWO content and expert tutorials, plus images and videos to help illustrate key points. Whether you're a total beginner or brushing up on advanced techniques, you'll find step-by-step insights to boost your wing foiling journey. Let's dive in!

Level 1 – Discovery Course

Your first steps into the exciting world of wing foiling

1A S.E.A, Gear, Setup, Safety

Learn the basics of wing foiling equipment, safety protocols, and proper setup techniques. This foundational knowledge is essential before getting on the water.

1B Beach Wing Handling, Use of Handles

Master the art of controlling your wing on land, understanding wind direction, and properly using the handles for maximum control and efficiency.

1C Beach Board Exercises, Knee Stand Up

Practice balance and board control on land, including the crucial transition from kneeling to standing position that you'll use in your early water sessions.

1D Beach Self Rescue, R.O.W.

Learn essential self-rescue techniques and the Rules of the Water (R.O.W.) to ensure your safety and the safety of others while wing foiling.

1E Flip Wing on Water, Enter and Exit with Full Gear

Master the technique of flipping your wing on water and practice safely entering and exiting the water with all your equipment.

Level 2 – Intermediate Course

Building confidence and control on the water

2F Knee on Board, Control Gear in Knees

Learn to balance on your board while kneeling and control your wing effectively from this position, a crucial stepping stone to standing up.

2G Ride on Knees, Change Direction

Master riding on your knees while controlling the wing, and practice changing direction smoothly while maintaining balance and control.

2H Ride Standing Up, Jibe with Wing/Feet

Take the exciting step of standing up on your board while riding, and learn to perform basic jibes by coordinating your wing and foot movements.

2I Ride Cross & Upwind, Change Direction

Develop the skills to ride across and against the wind, expanding your range and freedom on the water, while mastering direction changes.

Level 3 – Independent Course

Taking flight and experiencing the thrill of foiling

3J Pumping, Getting on the Foil

Learn the crucial technique of pumping to generate lift and get your board up on the foil, experiencing the magical feeling of flying above the water.

3K Control Direction & Speed Foiling

Master the art of controlling your direction and speed while foiling, developing the fine motor skills needed for smooth, controlled flight.

3L Toeside, Changing Foot

Learn to ride toeside and practice changing your foot position while maintaining balance and control on the foil.

3M Jibe, Tack

Master the techniques of jibing and tacking while on the foil, allowing you to change direction smoothly without losing your foiling state.

3N Catching Swell, Free Pumping

Develop the skills to catch and ride swells, and practice free pumping to maintain your foil without relying on the wing's power.

Level 4 – Advanced Course

Pushing boundaries with advanced techniques

4O Jump Basic

Learn the fundamentals of jumping with your wing foil, taking your first steps into the exciting world of aerial maneuvers.

4P Power Jibe

Master the dynamic power jibe, a more aggressive and efficient way to change direction while maintaining speed and control.

4Q Jump with Rotation

Take your jumping skills to the next level by adding rotations, adding style and flair to your aerial maneuvers.

4R Riding on Waves

Develop the skills to ride ocean waves with your wing foil, combining the thrill of surfing with the freedom of wing foiling.

4S Ride with Harness

Learn to use a harness while wing foiling, reducing arm fatigue and allowing for longer, more comfortable sessions.

4T Jump with Harness

Master the advanced technique of jumping while using a harness, combining two complex skills for maximum performance.
